RSS-NET results

The following table reports enrichment Bayes factors (BFs) of 38 inferred gene regulatory networks and 1 near-gene control network for a given trait. For each network, we evaluate BFs of the same baseline model (\(M_{0}\): \(\theta=0\) and \(\sigma^2=0\)) against 4 enrichment models (\(M_{1}\), \(M_{11}\), \(M_{12}\) and \(M_{13}\)). Rows are sorted by BFs based on \(M_{1}\) (the 2nd column).
