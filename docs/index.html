Quick assessments

Below are some quick assessments of PECA networks I received from Duren, using a simple likelihood ratio calculation introduced in Zhu and Stephens (2017). The aim of these quick assessments is to confirms whether these networks are informative in identifying disease-related tissues or cell types.

  • 201 PECA networks and 31 human complex traits
  • 73 PECA networks and 33 human complex traits
  • 73 PECA networks (TG only) and 33 human complex traits
  • 73 PECA networks (RE only) and 33 human complex traits
  • Comparison between TG or RE with full PECA networks
  • 66 MusDev lists and 33 human complex traits

This reproducible R Markdown analysis was created with workflowr 1.1.1
